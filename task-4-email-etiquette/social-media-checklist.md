Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for Students

✅ Do’s

Keep your profile respectful and professional
Share positive and helpful posts
Listen to and respect other people’s views
Use privacy settings to protect your information
Check facts before posting or sharing anything

❌ Don’ts

Avoid posting rude or inappropriate content
Do not share private or sensitive details
Never take part in cyberbullying
Do not spread false news or misinformation
