Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

Avoid clicking on suspicious links or fake discount messages.

Do not approve unknown UPI “collect requests” (remember, a PIN is only for sending money).

Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all your important accounts.

Do not trust calls claiming to be from your bank without hanging up and verifying the number yourself.

Never download remote access apps (like AnyDesk or TeamViewer) if a "customer support" agent asks you to.

Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank card details with anyone.

Lock your digital wallets and banking apps with a secure biometric lock or app PIN.

Always double-check the receiver's name and UPI ID before making any payment.

Call the cybercrime helpline number 1930 immediately for quick assistance if you suspect fraud.

Immediately report any cyber fraud on the official portal at cybercrime.gov.in.
